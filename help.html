Quickstart Guide

1. An Overview to Wowslides

Welcome to your new PowerPoint to Html5 converter! This is your presentation pocket. We want you to feel comfortable and share your awesome presentations with everybody in the world. So, we’ve put together this guide to help you to reach your Wow presentations to large masses.

Wowslides is an online PowePoint converter. It allows you to easily convert your PowerPoint presentation and PDF files to HTML5 content. Wowslides converts your presentations with all features when converting Html5 content such as animations, transition effects, triggers, embedded video and audio, tables, shapes, hyperlinks, buttons, smart art objects, text formatting, and effects etc.

Wowslides is available in three different usages: Free, Professional and Enterprise. You can visit the Wowslides Pricing Page for detailed usage information.

When you are logged in to Wowslides, you can always see the most important features at the top of the page: How It Works, Templates, Discover, Curators, Blog, Support.

If you want to access the account details it’s enough to click your profile picture in the top-right corner of the webpage. You can also view profile and settings tabs from this area to make desired edits for your account.

Learn more about Wowslides by visiting the How It Works? page.

You can download all the templates in Wowslides for free. On the Templates page, choose from among hundreds of templates that is best suited for your content needs, download and start editing as you wish. Now, upload your presentation to Wowslides. Convert it with all the features into digital materials, share them and follow up.

You can search for presentations on a topic you want by using the search box. If you want to follow trends and the last presentations, all you have to do is leave yourself to the flow of the Discover page.

Share your different ideas and practical knowledge on Blog page. Also, discover others blog posts and contribute them with your intimate knowledge.

Do you have a question? you can find answers to your questions by visiting our Help & Support and FAQ page. If you still can not find the answer you are looking for, do not hesitate to write to us.

Would you like to visitors have more information about you? Sounds like good, you can add your social media accounts to your Profile with a simple step. Start with uploading a profile photo and write something about yourself and your social media accounts’ links.

Explore in the Wowslides with a search bar, see your notifications instantly, and most importantly, upload your Wow presentations with the Upload button.

2. Upload Your Wow PowerPoint Presentations

You can upload your PowerPoint presentations as you logged in to system. Click Upload button and drag and drop files into the Wowslides from your desktop.

Notice: Note that if you click the upload button before you are not signed in to the system, Wowslides leads you to Login page.

Publishing Presentations

1. Upload

Wowslides, the most powerful converter, converts your PPTX file to HTML5 with its all features such as animations, transition effects, triggers, embedded video and audio, tables, shapes, hyperlinks, buttons, smart art objects, text formatting and effects etc.

How can I upload?

1. Sign in to wowslides.com.

2. Click Upload button.

3. Choose your PowerPoint presentation.

4. Fill in the relevant fields.

When you upload your presentation, the second step, editing project, will be opened automatically. On the left, you can preview your presentation. On the right, edit your presentation details. Enter a title, category, description and keywords.

Your presentation is ready to share.

What can I upload?

You can upload an unlimited number of PowerPoint presentation files that include unlimited number of slides formatted .ppt and .pptx. Also, you can upload PDF files.

2. Privacy Settings

Of course, privacy is as important to us as it is for you! Don’t worry. Wowslides provides you to arrange visibility settings of your presentation. Decide who you want your presentation to see. These privacy settings are:

Everyone: All users can view your presentation.

Only me: Only you can view your presentation.

Anyone with a link: Only ones who get your presentation link.

Anyone with a link & password: Only ones who get your presentation link and password.

CC by Share Alike > You let others copy, distribute, display, perform, and modify your work, as long as they distribute any modified work on the same terms. If they want to distribute modified works under other terms, they must get your permission first.

CC by Derivatives >You let others copy, distribute, display and perform only original copies of your work. If they want to modify your work, they must get your permission first.
