Frequently Asked Questions

GENERAL

What is Wowslides?

Wowslides is a powerful PowerPoint to HTML5 converter.

Where can I find the user guides of Wowslides?

Visit the user guide documentation page of the Wowslides.

Can I install Wowslides on my computer?

Wowslides is not a desktop application. It is a web application to convert and share your presentations online.

Which version of MS PowerPoint does Wowslides convert?

Wowslides converts all versions of MS PowerPoint presentations.

Is Wowslides free or paid?

Wowslides is a free application with all features. Only enterprise edition is paid.

Can I convert other file extensions except PowerPoint on Wowslides?

Yes. Wowslides converts ppt, pptx and pdf documents to html5.

Which features of PowerPoint presentation are converted to HTML5 by Wowslides?

Wowslides converts your PowerPoint presentations with all features such as animations, transitions, triggers, audios, videos, gifs, tables, shapes, hyperlinks, buttons, graphics, smart arts, text formatting and effects etc.

Can I upload a file without sign-in to Wowslides?

No, you must sign-in to Wowslides. This also provides you to analyze your presentations.

Is there a presentation limit for free membership?

No. You can upload how many presentations you want. But, if you want to embeddable private presentation, you should upgrade your membership to Wowslides Professional.

Is there a presentation limit for individual use?

No. You can upload how many presentations you want.

Is there a slide limitation for each presentation?

No. You can upload your presentation without limitation of slide number.

Does Wowslides play the video in my presentation?

Wowslides converts all features in a PowerPoint presentation. Your video will play on Wowslides.

Does Wowslides play the audio in my presentation?

Wowslides converts all features in a PowerPoint presentation. Your audio will play on Wowslides.

Does Wowslides view the gifs in my presentation?

Wowslides converts all features in a PowerPoint presentation. Your gifs will be viewed on Wowslides.

Do all presentations work on mobile?

Wowslides is a PowerPoint to Html5 converter. All presentations work flawlessly on all mobile devices.

TEMPLATES

Are the templates of Wowslides free or paid?

All templates of Wowslides are free for all users. You can download and start to use them on PowerPoint.

Are the templates of Wowslides compatible with all versions of MS PowerPoint?

No. Templates of Wowslides are compatible with only MS PowerPoint 2007 and over.

Can I publish my own template on Wowslides?

Yes, you can publish your own template public or private. When you upload your presentation, mark the “Save as a draft” option.

Click to see the guide of templates in detailed.

Please read the policy about templates before you share them with all users.

Can I sell my own template on Wowslides?

No. Wowslides is a free application except enterprises. It cannot be used for commercial purposes.

Is it required to confirm by Wowslides for publishing my own template?

Your template is submitted for the Wowslides’ approval. If it is confirmed, template will publish for members to use.

Can I remove a template that Wowslides confirmed before?

You can remove your published template.

Can I define specific users to download my template?

Uploaded templates are for all Wowslides users. You can’t define specific user for downloading your template.

PUBLISHING PRESENTATIONS

Who can view my presentation on Wowslides?

All Wowslides users can view your presentation. If you don’t want them to view it, you should use settings of your presentation. You can select one of the below:

Everyone: All users included non-member of Wowslides can view your presentation.

Members: All Wowslides members can view your presentation.

Private: Only you can view your presentation.

Click to see the guide of Publishing Settings in detailed.

Can I assign my presentation someone I defined?

You can use privacy settings to obtain link and password for your presentation. Or you can define domain restriction for your presentation. But, you should upgrade your membership to Wowslides Professional to use domain restriction.

Can users download my presentation?

If you allow users to download your presentation as PDF or PowerPoint, they can download it.

Can I download my presentation as a video?

Wowslides is a PowerPoint to HTML5 converter. It is not allowed to download a converted presentation.

Can I remove the default next and previous buttons when the presentation is viewed?

You can switch next and previous buttons, outline menu, laser and full screen options on presentation viewer settings.

ANALYTICS

Which reports of the presentation does Wowslides give?

Wowslides gives you reports that includes count of view and unique view, total watch time, user name, view dates and times, IP addresses, watched time and watched percentage per user who viewed your presentation.

Is analytic report of an updated presentation deleted?

Currently yes, we are working on it.

Is there any difference between presentation reports on Wowslides Professional and individual use?

Wowslides Enterprise and Wowslides Professional membership allows you to see more detailed user information who view your presentation than free account.

I signed up for Wowslides but I didn’t receive the confirmation email.

If you don’t see the confirmation e-mail, check your Spam folder and. If you’re using Gmail, please check the Social and Promotions folders.

I can’t remember my password for signing in. How do I reset it?

If you can’t remember your password, you can use the “I Forgot My Password. Send Again?” link under the “Password” on the Sign In page to reset it. Be sure to enter the same e-mail address as the one you have registered with Wowslides.

I can’t sign in after resetting my password.

When you try to sign in, be sure you’re using the same e-mail address that you used to reset when signing in to your account. Also, don’t forget that passwords are case-sensitive; make sure there are no extra spaces in the password field.
